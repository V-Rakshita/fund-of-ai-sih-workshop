COMPANY HR POLICY DOCUMENT

1. Working Hours
The standard working hours for employees are from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday. Employees are expected to log their attendance through the company attendance system.

2. Leave Policy
Employees are entitled to the following types of leave:

Casual Leave: 12 days per year.
Sick Leave: 10 days per year with medical proof if leave exceeds two consecutive days.
Earned Leave: 15 days per year that can be carried forward to the next year.
Maternity Leave: Female employees are eligible for 180 days of maternity leave as per government rules.

3. Work From Home Policy
Employees may work from home with manager approval. A maximum of 5 work-from-home days are allowed per month unless approved for special circumstances.

4. IT Support Policy
Employees facing issues with company systems, VPN access, or email login should contact the IT helpdesk through the internal portal or email support@company.com.

5. Password Policy
Employees must change their system password every 90 days. Passwords must contain at least 8 characters including numbers and special symbols.

6. Employee Conduct
Employees must maintain professional behavior within the organization. Harassment, discrimination, or use of abusive language is strictly prohibited.

7. Company Events
The company organizes the following annual events:
- Annual Tech Conference
- Employee Sports Day
- Cultural Fest
- New Year Celebration

8. Travel Reimbursement
Employees traveling for official purposes can claim reimbursement for travel, accommodation, and meals by submitting bills within 10 days of travel completion.

9. Dress Code
Employees are expected to maintain professional attire in the workplace. Casual wear is permitted on Fridays.

10. Grievance Redressal
Employees facing workplace issues can contact the HR department through the grievance portal or email hr@company.com. All complaints will be handled confidentially.
